commuting
commuting provides users with real-time location of vehicles of various public transit agencies around the Bay Area to visualize how convenient and accessible getting around with public transportation can be!
Created with Node/express and React. Utilizing GTFS technology, MapBox, and the 511.org developer API!
Live Link
CollegeLookup
CollegeLookup provides future collegiates and their families with easily accessible and understandable information to help them with their college selection process!
Created with Ruby on Rails and React/Redux. Data from the IPEDS database provided by the National Center for Education Statistics. Visualized using the Semiotic library
Live Link
CliqueUp
CliqueUp is a MeetUp clone that allows users to meet like-minded people who share the same interests and hobbies! Users can create and join their own groups, create or rsvp for group events, and track group member info.
Created with Ruby on Rails and React/Redux
Live Link
